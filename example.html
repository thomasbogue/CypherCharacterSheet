Dorren is a Cruel Unseelie Explorer who Awakens Dreams
Special Abilities
Natural Magic (1 Intellect Point, Action)

In any plane, you can summon "small magics". somtimes called cantrips. You can light candles, draw a cupful of water from the air, life and move small objects, cleanse objects, color objects, and more. Effects generally last no longer than the next sunrise/sunset. In the Fae realm, you can do more. For example, you can create small objects (maximum level 1), pulling them from thin air; intensify or calm local weather; and cause a plant to grow, blossom, or wither. You can not create a plant from nothing (must have at least a seed to start with) and you can not create a living creature. You can not use Magic in the Blood to harm any creature directly or to destroy an object directly.

Cruelty (enabler)

When you use force, you can choose to maim or deliver painful injuries to draw out your foe's suffering. Whenever you inflict damage, you can choose to inflict 2 fewer points of damage to ease your next attack against that foe.

Skills
  • Trained in deception, intimidation, and persuasion when dealing iwth characters experiencing physical or emotional pain
  • Trained in intellect defense tasks
  • Trained in tasks related to sleep
  • Trained in mixing natural elixers to help creatures fall asleep
  • Trained in the lore of the Fae lands and the Courts
  • Practiced with light and medium weapons
  • Hindered in any task to ascertain another character's feelings, motives, or disposition
  • Hindered with heavy weapon attacks
